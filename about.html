Our Vision & Mission

Introducing the Virtual Metro Card

At Hifi Technologies, we are proud to present our revolutionary Virtual Metro card. Our unique system provides fast and secure access to every metro passenger with a goal to establish One Nation, One Card and One QR.

Mission

We want to eliminate the need of metro travellers to think about purchasing tickets as soon as they reach metro stations, instead of having queue at the ticket counters or access third party applications to input their travel details such as origin & destination, we aim to simplify the entire process for the passengers.

Valuing each step of Passengers' journey

Integrating gamification provides users to gain benefits on each travel and more other incentives. Elimination of the mandotary purchase of paying for the card. Convenient digital deliveries of card.
